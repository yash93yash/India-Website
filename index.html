About India

India is a vast and diverse country located in South Asia. It is the world's second-most populous country, with over 1.4 billion people. The capital of India is New Delhi, and the official languages are Hindi and English. India is a federal parliamentary democratic republic, with a President as the head of state and a Prime Minister as the head of government. India has a rich history, being one of the oldest civilizations in the world. It is home to ancient cultures, including the Indus Valley Civilization. The country was ruled by various dynasties, such as the Mauryas, Guptas, and Mughals, before gaining independence from British rule in 1947. India is known for its diverse religions, including Hinduism, Islam, Christianity, Sikhism, Buddhism, and Jainism.
